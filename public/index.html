MYSTERY HISTORY

Made by Daniel Madan










What is Mystery History?

A social game where a group of 3-12 players submit an array of facts about themselves and subsequently try to guess who submitted each set over the course of several rounds. Points are awarded based on how fast each player is able to correctly guess who submitted the current series of facts.

How to play

Use the following steps to set up the game on your device:

  1. On the device that you would like to host the game with, click here to go to the game's itch.io page. Click play and begin a session to have the game create a room for you.
  2. From this website, all players will set their names and join the room using the displayed room ID. Once every player has joined the room, click the Continue button.
  3. Wait for every player who submit at least 10 facts about themselves with the specified requirements. When every player has submitted their facts, click the Start button.
